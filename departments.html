Departments

Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)

The Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) department stands as the cornerstone of technological advancement within the academic realm. Renowned for its commitment to excellence, it offers a comprehensive curriculum that encompasses a spectrum of computer science disciplines. Students delve into the intricacies of algorithms, artificial intelligence, databases, and cybersecurity, gaining a profound understanding of these foundational principles. The department prides itself on fostering an environment where theoretical knowledge converges seamlessly with practical applications. Faculty members, often distinguished experts in their fields, spearhead cutting-edge research initiatives. This commitment to innovation ensures that students are exposed to the latest advancements, preparing them for the dynamic landscape of the tech industry. Beyond traditional coursework, the CSE department encourages hands-on learning through collaborative projects, hackathons, and internships. These experiences not only enrich students' problem-solving skills but also provide a glimpse into real-world scenarios. The CSE department serves as a crucible for intellectual curiosity and innovation. Its graduates emerge not only with a robust academic foundation but also with the adaptability and creativity needed to thrive in the rapidly evolving tech sector. As a hub of technological ingenuity, the CSE department is instrumental in shaping the next generation of professionals who will leave an indelible mark on the world of computer science.

In the era of big data, the AI-DS department plays a pivotal role in shaping the future workforce. As a crucible for innovation, the AI-DS department is instrumental in cultivating the skills and mindset necessary for professionals to navigate and contribute to the transformative landscape of artificial intelligence and data science.
